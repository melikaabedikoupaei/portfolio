About Me

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Industrial Engineering from one of the top universities in Iran. After graduation, I began my career as an Industrial Engineer at a wood factory, where I played a pivotal role as the ERP Project Manager. In this position, I successfully implemented an ERP system(Odoo), created comprehensive reports for the factory, in logistics, costs, and other fields. After a year and a half, having completed the ERP project at the wood factory, I sought to broaden my knowledge and experience by working for a leading ERP company in Iran. Over the next three years, I had the opportunity to work with over 75 different companies and organizations. I assisted them in implementing warehouse and manufacturing modules, and provided business intelligence reports to streamline their production processes. This extensive exposure to various industries and data-driven projects fueled my interest in data science. To further my expertise, I decided to pursue a Master's degree in Data Science at Ghent University. Armed with both practical experience and advanced academic knowledge, I am now eager to tackle new challenges and continue my journey in the field of data science.
