E-Commerce Data Insights

1. Situation

I worked with Belgian e-commerce company data to identify factors affecting order delays, customer purchasing behavior, and seller performance.

In this project, I worked with a dataset from a Belgian e-commerce company. The data included information on orders, customer locations, seller performance, and customer comments. The company aimed to understand order delays, customer buying patterns, and the impact of seller performance on delays.

2. Task

My goal was to uncover insights from the data that could help improve seller performance, understand customer behavior, and identify patterns in order delays.

My tasks included identifying key sellers causing delays, clustering customers based on their RFM metrics, analyzing peak purchase times, and creating a word cloud to visualize customer feedback.

3. Action

I employed several analytical techniques and tools to complete these tasks:

  • Identified Key Sellers: Analyzed order delay data to pinpoint the 34 sellers responsible for 80% of the delays.
  • Customer Clustering: Used RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis to segment customers into clusters for targeted marketing strategies.
  • Purchase Patterns: Analyzed purchase data to find peak hours and days for customer transactions.
  • Customer Feedback Analysis: Created a word cloud from customer comments to visualize common feedback themes.

I used Python libraries such as Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation and analysis. Applied clustering algorithms and visualized results using Matplotlib and Seaborn. Created a word cloud using the WordCloud library to analyze and visualize customer comments. Conducted a time series analysis to identify peak purchasing hours and days.

4. Result

The analysis provided actionable insights that helped the company improve operations and marketing strategies.

Identified 34 sellers who were responsible for 80% of the delays, allowing the company to focus on improving their performance. Successfully clustered customers into distinct groups, enabling more targeted marketing strategies. Discovered peak purchase hours and days, which informed better inventory and marketing planning. The word cloud analysis revealed common customer concerns, which led to actionable improvements in service quality.
