AI-Based Job Recommendation System

1. Situation

Many job seekers struggle to find suitable job opportunities that align with their skills and experience. Traditional job search methods often require manual filtering, making it difficult to identify relevant jobs efficiently. My goal was to build an AI-powered system that could automatically recommend personalized jobs based on the user's resume content.

2. Task

The task was to create a system that allowed users to upload their resumes and receive tailored job recommendations. This involved developing an NLP model to parse the resume, analyze its content, and compare it to job descriptions in the database using advanced machine learning techniques, such as BERT embeddings and cosine similarity.

3. Action

To build this system, I followed these steps:

  • Data Collection: Gathered job descriptions and their requirements from various sources and created a structured database.
  • Resume Parsing: Developed a resume cleaning and parsing pipeline using NLP libraries (e.g., NLTK, spaCy) to extract meaningful content from uploaded resumes.
  • Embedding Generation: Leveraged the DistilBERT model to generate vector representations (embeddings) for both resumes and job descriptions.
  • Similarity Matching: Implemented cosine similarity to compare the resume embeddings with job descriptions and rank the most relevant job opportunities.
  • Model Evaluation: Evaluated the system by comparing the quality of job recommendations against manually curated lists, ensuring that the most suitable jobs were recommended.
4. Result

The job recommendation system was able to match user resumes with job opportunities with high accuracy. Users received personalized job suggestions that significantly reduced the time and effort spent on searching for relevant positions. The system identified the best matching jobs with over 85% accuracy, leading to increased user engagement and satisfaction.
