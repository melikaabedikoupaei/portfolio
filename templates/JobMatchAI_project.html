AI-Based Job Recommendation System

1. Situation

Job seekers often face challenges in finding job opportunities that match their skills and experience. Traditional job search methods require significant manual effort and may not yield the best results. This project addresses this problem by leveraging AI to automate and enhance the job matching process.

2. Task

The goal was to develop a system where users can upload their resumes and receive personalized job recommendations. The system needed to efficiently process resume content, compare it with job descriptions, and provide relevant job suggestions using advanced NLP and machine learning techniques.

3. Action

The project was executed with the following steps:

  • Resume Parsing: Implemented a pipeline to convert resume files to text and extract key information using NLP libraries like spaCy and NLTK.
  • Job Scraping: Developed a scraper to gather job descriptions from Indeed, ensuring a diverse and up-to-date dataset.
  • Translation: Integrated Google Translate API to handle non-English job descriptions and translate them to English.
  • Similarity Calculation: Used spaCy to compute cosine similarity between resume content and job descriptions, providing relevance scores for job matches.
  • Evaluation: Assessed the effectiveness of job recommendations by comparing them with manually curated job lists, achieving high accuracy in matching relevant jobs.
4. Result

The system effectively matched user resumes with job opportunities, offering highly relevant job suggestions. The solution improved the job search process by automating relevance assessment and reducing the time spent by users in finding suitable job positions. The project demonstrated a significant improvement in job matching accuracy and user satisfaction.
